There is **no single national Section 8 application**. You apply through the **Public Housing Authority (PHA)** that serves your county or metro area. Many PHAs only open waiting lists for a few days every few years.

## Typical steps

1. **Check income** against HUD limits for your household size — use our [calculator](/calculator/) with your ZIP.
2. **Find your PHA** on HUD’s [PHA contact list](https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/public_indian_housing/pha/contacts).
3. **Apply when the waiting list opens** — online, mail, or in person per PHA instructions.
4. **Wait for selection** — can be months to many years depending on area.
5. **Receive a voucher** and search for a qualifying rental before the voucher term expires.

## While you wait

- Keep contact info updated with the PHA.
- Ask about **local preferences** (elderly, disabled, veteran, working family).
- Learn your area’s **payment standard** so you know what rent range to expect.
