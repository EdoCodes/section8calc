The **Housing Choice Voucher (HCV)** program is the main form of **Section 8** help for renters in private apartments and houses. HUD funds it; **Public Housing Authorities (PHAs)** run waiting lists and payments in each area.

## What the voucher does

- You pay a **tenant share** toward rent and utilities (Total Tenant Payment).
- The voucher pays the landlord the rest, up to the local **payment standard** (based on Fair Market Rent).
- You choose a unit that meets PHA rent and inspection rules.

## Not the same as public housing

| Program | Where you live |
| --- | --- |
| **Housing Choice Voucher** | Private market rental you find |
| **Public housing** | Government-owned developments |
| **Project-based Section 8** | Specific buildings with attached subsidy |
